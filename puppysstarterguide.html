GP Making

  • Skilling Tasks: Visit Fritz the Skiller (east of Home teleport)
  • Select tasks for any skill you meet level requirements for
  • Reroll unwanted tasks by clicking "Get Task" again (for a fee)
  • Rewards: 100k–400k+ GP per task
  • Earn Skilling Points to spend in the Skilling Shop
Each outfit in the shop gives 25 Achievement Points
  • Thieving: Pickpocket NPCs for GP (prioritize obtaining the Rogue's Outfit)
  • Ardougne Knights (trapped in buildings) are highly AFK-friendly

Voting / Vote Shop

  • Vote every 12 hours via ;;vote
Critical Tip: DO NOT claim votes until unlocking the Point Harvester relic (doubles banked votes)
  • Stackable with occasional "Double Vote" server boosts
  • Vote Shop Strategy:
  • 75 votes = $5 Bond (grants 5 dono tier points + 50 dono shop credits)
  • Priority: Bonds > most Vote Shop items (many duplicates in Dono Shop)

Where to Find Things

  • Shops at Home: Talk to any Shop Merchant → navigate via tabs
  • Exception: Tynan (Fishing Shop, south of Home) sells feathers!
  • General Shop (west of Home, across bridge):
  • Zahur: Decants potions
  • Blue Wizard: Imbues gear (see Combat Points)
  • AFK Training: Cows: Teleporter > Training > Cows (Lumbridge cow pen)

Contracts

  • Location: Dr. Banikan (west of Home, across bridge)
  • Rewards: Combat Points, XP, GP, and rare items
  • Types: Daily tasks + 1 weekly task
Combat Points are rare and essential!

Combat Points

  • Uses: Imbue Black Masks, Slayer Helmets, Salve Amulets, Rings, and Iban Staff
  • Only method for imbuing!
  • NPC: Blue Wizard in General Shop (west of Home)
  • Earning Methods:
  • Combat Achievements
  • Barrows runs
  • Slaying superior monsters
  • Contracts
  • Exchanging Ancient Artifacts (from Revenants)

Relics

  • Access: Character Summary → Purple Kourend Favour tab → Relics/Masteries
  • Plan carefully! Resets cost votes/dono points later
  • Unlocking: Complete tasks (view via Tasks icon)
  • Starter Guide: Aheiya's Relic Guide

Tier Recommendations:

T1
Kano Pick (fast early tasks)
T2
Finders Keepers > Skillers Galore (skilling crates > passive GP)
T3
Point Harvester (doubles votes/skilling/Slayer/Pest Control points)
T4
Slayer Mastermind (choose Slayer tasks)
T5
Blood Alchemy (doubles enchanting orb drops → high GP)
T6
Combat Artisan (popular QoL)

Relic Tasks I Liked

  • Milk Cows (AFK/overnight):
    • 100/1k/10k/50k milks → 185 total points
  • Craft Gems (fast points):
    • Buy 1,250 uncut sapphires/emeralds/rubies/diamonds (Al Kharid Gem Shop)
    • Cut all → 830 points

Combat Masteries

  • Unlocks: Attack speed doubling + powerful combat buffs (view in Relics tab)
  • Earning: Complete tasks
  • Examples:
  • Defeat an enemy in full Void
  • Kill 10+ monsters with Combat Lv. 100+
  • Achieve 55 Slayer
  • Defeat "any boss"

Enchantments

  • Orb Drops: Annexing, Faltering, Turmoil, Annulment (from monsters)
  • Mechanics (PoE-inspired)
  • Enchanting Bowl: West-south of Home (near Fritz the Skiller)
  • Process: Place gear (non-ammo) + orbs in the interface

Orb Types:

  • Annex: Adds 1 new random enchantment (from right-side list)
  • Turmoil: Adds/replaces 3 random enchantments
  • Falter: Rerolls tiers of existing enchants (may alter god-type enchants)
  • Annul: Removes 1 random enchantment

Enchantment List: kano Enchantments Guide
